Our Mission

Elevating Latino Voices in American Politics

Latino Leaders 2026 is dedicated to spotlighting young Latino candidates who are stepping up to serve their communities. We believe representation matters, and the next generation of leaders is ready to make their mark.

Our platform connects voters with candidates who share their values and understand their communities. From local councils to Congress, these leaders are building a more inclusive democracy.

Why It Matters

The Power of Representation

62M+
Latinos in the U.S.

The Latino community is the largest ethnic minority, yet remains underrepresented in elected offices at every level.

19%
Of U.S. Population

Latinos make up nearly 1 in 5 Americans, and that number continues to grow every year.

36M
Eligible Voters

Latino voters have the power to shape elections at every level, from local school boards to the presidency.

<10%
Of Congress

Despite their population share, Latinos hold less than 10% of seats in the U.S. Congress. It's time to change that.
